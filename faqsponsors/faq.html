Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is the schedule for ProSang 2020?

“The dates for ProSang-2020 are from 21st February 2020 to 23rd February 2020.

2. How do I register for an event or a workshop in ProSang 2020?

“On the official website, when you will open the event, you will find the register option on the same.

3. How to reach the campus on arrival at Allahabad?

“You can reach Allahabad by train, air or by bus. Once you reach Allahabad, you will have to take an auto (private/shared) from either the Bus Stand or the Junction (wherever you are!) to Teliyarganj and here you will have to enter through the Yamuna or the Ganga Gate in the college.

4. What about my accommodations at the institute?

“For all the qualified students, accommodation will be provided at MNNIT Campus only.

5. Is food also included in the accommodation?

“No, the food is not included in the accommodation. But there are food court, canteens, hostel messes where you can pay a nominal amount and get the food.

6. What kind of accommodation is provided?

“Accommodation is provided on a shared basis inside campus hostels or outside hotels. One mattress, and enough space for keeping luggage, and other essential commodities will be provided. Girls and boys will be accommodated separately. Number of guests in a room will be decided by Prosang and will be allotted by Prosang Team.

7. What shall be the duration of my stay?

“You will get accommodation for 3 days from 21st Feb 2020 (Friday) noon to 23rd Feb 2020 (Monday) noon.

8. Shall I carry Id Card with me?

“It is mandatory for all guest participants to carry college IDs. This is for your own convenience. Moreover, you will be asked to produce your college ID cards at the time of allotment of rooms.

9. What about medical facilities on the campus?

“We have a Health Centre on the campus (known as HC) with all the medical facilities.

10. What about the security inside the campus?

“We have a private security agency called SIS active all 24 hours. Its job is to safeguard the campus against thefts and see to the security of the hostels.

11. What kind of discipline is expected from me?

“MNNIT is known for its strict discipline and the respect that the campus community has for its culture. Though we in no way stop you from completely enjoying the festival, we at least expect from you to honor the MNNIT tradition. Please do not bring liquor or any other intoxicating stuff inside the campus and do not be involved in hooliganism and rowdiness.

12. What are the Latest Updates and how will I know them?

“The pages of all the events are being updated continuously with the latest information, day-after-day. The Latest Updates scrolling is also shown on the top of every page.
